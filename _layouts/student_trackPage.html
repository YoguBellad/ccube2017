Features of Student Track


  • Submitted papers will be thoroughly reviewed and checked for plagiarism
  • Accepted papers will be printed in the Conference Proceedings (available in hard copy and online)
  • International Conference Certification provided to paper registrants and presenters
  • Poster presentations are also encouraged
  • Product/ Project demonstrations are welcome (prior intimation with regard to requirements of power, display, etc to be given)
  • Best paper award of Rs. 5000

NOTE: Student Track papers are not uploaded into IEEE Xplore
